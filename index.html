Pete Kowalczyk

My work has been published by the Guardian, Dazed, CNN, Vice, the Nikkei and Latterly, broadcast on CNN Connect the world, and screened at South London Shorts and DIY Cultures. I studied poetry at Goldsmiths, worked with the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees, and have been asked to speak at the Institute of Contemporary Arts in London — pete.kowalczyk@gmail.com
